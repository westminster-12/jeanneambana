Project information

This game is a First-Person Shooter (FPS) game for the final project of the Computer Graphics class at UNKLAB. It draws inspiration from the cultural traditions of Bali, including traditional houses such as Klumpu Jineng, Aling-Aling, Bale Dauh, and the iconic beach atmosphere of Bali's tourist areas. The game consists of 2 levels that follow the story of the main character, Malika, a hero attempting to save Bali from colonial invaders. In the second level, Malika teams up with her partner, Panji, to assist in completing missions. The enemies are called Yakuza. In addition to battling enemies, Malika must collect checkpoints located in specific locations.

  • Editor: Unity, Visual Studio
  • Language: Written In C#
  • Character modelling: Adobe Fuse
  • Object modelling: Sketchup, Blender
  • Additional assets:: FPS Assets - Unity Store (for Character Animation), Terrain Assets, Unity Menu (Main Menu interface), Youtube audio (checkpoints audio)
