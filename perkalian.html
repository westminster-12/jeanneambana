Belajar Perkalian

I developed an engaging multiplication learning application aimed at elementary school students while working at the Ministry of Education, in collaboration with the Learning Technology Development Team (Pengembang Teknologi Pembelajaran). The application features unique animations and easy-to-understand multiplication explanations, designed to capture and retain the interest of young learners. Inspired by the popular "Among Us" theme, which is widely loved by children, the application provides an interactive and enjoyable learning experience.

Key Features:

  • Engaging Animations: Unique animations to make learning fun and interesting.
  • Child-Friendly Explanations: Simplified multiplication concepts tailored for elementary school students.
  • Popular Themes:: "Among Us" themed design to attract and engage children.
  • Cross-Platform Support: Available on both Android and Windows platforms for broader accessibility.

Technologies Used

  • C#, Unity
